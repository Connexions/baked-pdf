What role do electrons play in dehydration synthesis and hydrolysis?
  1. Sharing of electrons between monomers occurs in both dehydration synthesis and hydrolysis.
  2. The sharing of electrons between monomers occurs in hydrolysis only.
  3. \text{H}^+ and \text{OH}^- ions share electrons with the respective monomers in dehydration synthesis.
  4. \text{H}^+ and \text{OH}^- ions share electrons with the respective monomers in hydrolysis.
